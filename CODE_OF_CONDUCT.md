# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as
contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project and
its community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Being welcoming to people with different backgrounds and experience levels
- Accepting responsibility and learning from mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory comments
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may take
appropriate and fair corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

- Issues and pull requests
- Project discussions
- Community channels linked from this repository

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private security/advisory report where possible,
or contact the maintainer listed in `CREDITS.md`.
